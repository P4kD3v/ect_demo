Endometrial Cancer Tool (Demo) description

The Endometrial Cancer Tool (Demo) is a cutting-edge web application designed for researchers, clinicians, and epidemiologists to statistically analyze this disease survival data and generate comprehensive survival plots.

This intuitive platform simplifies the complex process of survival analysis, making it accessible to users with varying levels of statistical expertise. Users can easily create plots with no need of previous bioinformatic or statistics knowledge.

The core feature of ECT (Demo) is its powerful Kaplan-Meier estimator, which allows users to visualize survival functions over time. Through an easy-to-use interface, users can customize survival plots by selecting variables such as grade, hystologic type, molecular subtype and stage.

The application also supports advanced statistical methods, including Mann-Witney U, Hazard Ratio and log-rank p-value tests, to compare survival curves and assess the impact of covariates on survival outcomes. Also it offers dynamic and interactive visualizations, where users can adjust time scales, zoom in, zoom out or select an area to study, enabling users to explore data effectively.

Additionally, the platform supports the export of high-resolution plots, made with Dash/Plotly, suitable for publications or presentations.
