Purpose

The purpose of this tool is to complement the research in Uterine Corpus Endometrial Carcinoma (UCEC) giving tools to make a statistical analysis of their data, to have an overview of the effects that this type of cancer produces in people depending some different aspects as can be the patient age, if the patient recieved radiation therapy or not, the expresion of one gene, and more.

Data Sets

In order to do a survival analysis of this type of cancer, we where useing the Uterine Corpus Endometrial Carcinoma (TCGA, PanCancer Atlas) dataset from cBioportal (Reference: TCGA, Cell 2018) with 529 patient samples.
